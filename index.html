About

Shawna Carey is a visual artist specializing in surrealism art based on

At a young age, Shawna began her journey as a visual artist through the Jefferson Parish Talented Visual Arts Program in Metairie, LA. This program helped to cultivate Shawna’s curiosity through various mediums: pottery, photography, graphite, cray pas, colored pencils, charcoal, etc...

Shawna’s formal talented art classes ended after graduating from McKinley Senior High in Baton Rouge, LA., but her desire to create visual art never ceased. This brought Shawna to create art stimulated purely from her personal experiences. ​
